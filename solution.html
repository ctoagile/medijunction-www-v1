Health Programs Powered by Medijunction

Each program runs on the same platform with its own branding, subdomain, role labels, and integration credentials. Complete data isolation between programs.

GCHC

Girls as Community Health Coordinators

Our flagship program empowering young women in rural Uttar Pradesh as community health coordinators. They conduct health screenings, facilitate teleconsultations, manage patient records, and provide follow-up care in villages with limited access to healthcare facilities.

Visit Medijunction Platform +

MediStudent

Medical Education & Clinical Training

A platform for medical students and interns to practice clinical workflows, patient intake, and case management. Provides a safe training environment with simulated patient data, AI-assisted learning, and structured progression from observer to practitioner.

Visit MediStudent +

Rural Health Connect

Primary Healthcare Delivery Network

Connecting primary health centres (PHCs) and sub-centres in rural districts with a unified digital platform. Enables systematic patient tracking, referral management, and health outcome monitoring across a network of healthcare facilities.

Visit Rural Health Connect +

Corporate Wellness

Employee Health & Preventive Care

A comprehensive employee wellness program for organizations. Provides health screenings, teleconsultation with specialists, mental health support, and preventive care tracking — all through the same trusted Medijunction platform.

Getting Started

How to Launch Your Health Program

Setting up a new health program on the Medijunction platform takes just four steps.

  • Step 1: Register your program with custom branding & subdomain
  • Step 2: Configure integrations (WhatsApp, SMS, ABDM, AI)
  • Step 3: Onboard health workers with phone-first auth
  • Step 4: Go live with intake, triage, teleconsultation & reminders
