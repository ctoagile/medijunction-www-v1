Our Story

Founded to Make Quality Healthcare Accessible in Rural India

Medijunction Smart Healthcare Private Limited was founded with a singular mission: to bridge the healthcare gap between urban and rural India. Based in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, we recognized that millions of people in rural communities lack access to basic primary healthcare — not because solutions don't exist, but because they aren't designed for the realities of these communities.

Our team combines deep expertise in healthcare delivery, software engineering, and community development to build technology that works in the real world — offline-capable, multi-language, and designed for first-time smartphone users.

  • Community-first approach
  • Technology for last-mile delivery
  • Government compliance (ABDM, UHI)
  • Open, multi-tenant architecture

Our Mission

To make affordable primary healthcare accessible everywhere through technology, community empowerment, and AI-assisted workflows that bridge the rural-urban health divide.

Our Vision

Every village in India connected to quality healthcare. A future where geography is no longer a barrier to receiving timely, compassionate, and competent medical care.

Our Values

Health equity, community ownership, technological simplicity, data privacy, and measurable impact. We design for the most constrained environments and work upward from there.

Key Differentiators

What sets our platform apart from traditional healthcare IT solutions

ABDM & UHI Compliant

Fully integrated with India's Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission and Unified Health Interface. ABHA ID, FHIR R4, consent-based data sharing, and Beckn protocol support.

Offline-First Design

Progressive Web App with IndexedDB sync engine and service workers. Health workers can collect data, do triage, and manage patients even without internet connectivity.

AI-Assisted, Human-in-Loop

Claude AI provides risk assessment and case preparation, but never makes autonomous clinical decisions. All AI outputs require human validation before action.

Multi-Language Support

Full Hindi and English support with i18n-ready architecture. Role labels, UI text, and notifications adapt to the user's preferred language.

Multi-Tenant Architecture

One platform, multiple health programs. Each program gets its own branding, subdomain, role configuration, and integration credentials — complete data isolation.

Phone-First Authentication

Designed for rural India where email isn't common. OTP-based login via SMS and WhatsApp, with optional Google sign-in for urban users.
