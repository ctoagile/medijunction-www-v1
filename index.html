Getting Started

How To Launch Your Health Program

Our multi-tenant platform lets you set up and configure a new community health program in days, not months.

1. Register Your Program

Create your program on the Medijunction platform with custom branding, role labels, and subdomain. Configure your specific workflows and health focus areas.

2. Configure the Platform

Set up integrations — WhatsApp, SMS, ABDM, AI providers — with per-program credentials. Customize role hierarchies and access controls.

3. Onboard Health Workers

Register community health coordinators with phone-first authentication. Train them using the platform's built-in progression from learner to senior coordinator.

4. Go Live

Start serving patients with offline-capable intake, AI triage, teleconsultation, digital prescriptions, and automated follow-up reminders.
